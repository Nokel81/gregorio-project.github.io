Submit Your Own

Do you have a tip or trick that you'd like to see featured here? The easiest way to submit it is to fork the website repository, create a page for your submission and then submit a pull request. Don't have a GitHub account? You can sign-up for free, or submit your tip or trick to the developer's mailing list.

Please keep in mind that a good tip or trick doesn't just tell the reader to use one of the documented settings. It should go beyond, using them in some interesting way or combining them with something else to produce something different.
