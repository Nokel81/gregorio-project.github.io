Here are some interesting links about Gregorian chant:

  • Virtual library of the abbey at Saint Gall. Here, with a little searching, one can see wonderful ancient scores of Gregorian chant. One of the best resources.
  • Study on the Bellelay Gradual where we can consult images of the original 12th century Norbertine manuscript. Very interesting.
  • Gregoriofacsimil, a site containing a lot of manuscripts and a very impressive collection of links.
  • the priory of Nidauzel, offering music downloads under a free licence.
